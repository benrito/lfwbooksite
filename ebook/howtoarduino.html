STEP ONE

  • Find a project that you'd like to try. Step-by-step tutorials can be found at: www.arduino.cc www.sparkfun.com www.adafruit.com blog.makezine.com/archive/arduino http://hacknmod.com/topics/arduino/ http://arduinofun.com/ http://www.instructables.com/ or by searching Google.

STEP TWO

  • Download and install the Arduino software to your computer. Instructions are here.

STEP THREE

  • Wire the physical parts together. Follow the photos in the tutorial. You may need extras like LED lights, jumper wires, USB cables, or "shields" which are boards that can be plugged in on top of the original Arduino, allowing it to do more stuff.

STEP FOUR

  • Open the project "sketch" on your computer.

STEP FIVE

  • Modify the sketch. Add comments to explain what you are doing.

STEP SIX

  • Compile the sketch.

STEP SEVEN

  • Upload the sketch.

STEP EIGHT

  • Test the program, troubleshoot, and repeat.
