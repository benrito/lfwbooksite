HOW TO
Create & award a badge


Create badges to award participants in your community,
or to show off your own learning.

Difficulty

Moderate
(sliding scale).

Time

1 day to create;
several weeks
to use.

Who

1 person

Materials

Computer with Internet access, a drawing program such as Inkscape:

Link
STEP ONE

  • Decide what your badge will be for. Tip: Consider linking it to a discrete, concrete achievement (like baking bread) or a positive behavior valued by a community (like leaving five comments on a user forum, or giving helpful feedback on a classmates' or colleague's presentation).

STEP TWO

  • Create a fun name (Chef Supreme) and recognizable symbol for your badge. Make this in a form of a button they can post on their profile.

STEP THREE

  • Make clear how the badge will be verified (honor system, link to some proof, such as a photo, or community votes up/down.)

STEP FOUR

  • Start awarding or nominating people for the badge. Give them a lot of props!

STEP FIVE

  • Publish these steps so others can add to your badge system.
